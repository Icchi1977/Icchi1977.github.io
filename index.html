Introduction

Before you start business in Japan, you need to know the Japanese legal system. In particular, you should know what laws apply to you and your business.

You may have no idea on which type of business entity is appropriate for your busness or investment.

This website is aimed at supporting foreing companies and investors to make a start of their business and investment in Japan.
